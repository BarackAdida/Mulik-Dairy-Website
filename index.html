Welcome to Mulik Dairy

Our Story

Mulik Dairy Solutions and Kanjiru Dairy Limited were founded with a single goal in mind: to improve the livelihoods of smallholder dairy farmers in Kenya. We understood the challenges that farmers face—limited access to resources, lack of financing, and low-quality inputs—and we set out to change that. Our journey began with a simple yet impactful idea: support farmers not just by buying their milk but by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to succeed. Since our inception, we’ve worked tirelessly to develop a comprehensive approach to dairy farming that blends the best of traditional practices with modern innovations. Through our dedication to excellence, we’ve built strong partnerships, engaged in community-building efforts, and helped farmers produce better yields, healthier cows, and stronger businesses

Our Services

  • Dairy Aggregation

    • We collect high-quality milk from smallholder farmers daily and supply it to larger processing companies such as Brookside. Through our robust milk aggregation system, we ensure that farmers are paid on time, every time, giving them peace of mind and a consistent income stream.

  • Input Financing

    • Through our unique input financing model, Mulik Dairy Solutions provides farmers with the feeds and supplies they need to care for their cows, delivered on credit. This enables farmers to access critical resources without the burden of upfront costs, allowing them to focus on growing their business and improving productivity.

  • Veterinary Services

    • We understand that the health of livestock is crucial to a farm’s success. That’s why we provide veterinary services to ensure cows are well taken care of, preventing disease and maximizing milk production

  • Training and Education

    • To help farmers improve their practices, we offer training and educational resources on topics like milk quality, herd management, and sustainable farming techniques. Our goal is to equip farmers with the knowledge they need to grow their businesses and improve productivity.
